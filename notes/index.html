DBN is the product of many people. Ben Fry is the chief architect of the most current release. The previous release, DBN 1.0.1 was created by Tom White. The original version DBN 1.0 was created by John Maeda.

Concurrently we have several DBN-related developments. A DBN Courseware system built by Casey Reas, a user site by Lauren Dubick, and a cast of DBN workshop material that has been administered in Seoul, Tokyo, LA, New York, and Cambridge, MA. Other people that have contributed to DBN development are Peter Cho, Elise Co, Golan Levin, Jocelyn Lin, and Josh Nimoy.
